**Last updated:** July 2026

## Ownership

The "Norm." name, the Norm. logo, the badge/emblem, and the overall visual identity ("Norm. Marks") are the exclusive property of the Norm. Project and its official distribution.

## Open Source ≠ Open Brand

While the Norm. source code is licensed under AGPLv3 (see `LICENSE`), the Norm. Marks are **not** covered by that license. Permission to use the Marks is granted only to:

1. **Official releases** — Binaries and installers distributed via the project's official GitHub Releases page.
2. **Fork attribution** — Forks of the source code may retain the Norm. name only in the original copyright notice and must clearly label themselves as "unofficial" or "forked" in all user-facing contexts.
3. **Community use** — Non-commercial references (e.g., articles, tutorials, reviews) may use the Marks nominatively to refer to the project, provided they do not imply endorsement.

## Prohibited Uses

- **Misleading distribution** — You may not distribute modified versions of Norm. under the "Norm." name in app stores, package managers, or any channel where users could reasonably mistake them for the official product.
- **Logo modification** — You may not alter, recolor, or combine the Norm. logo with other elements in a way that dilutes the brand.
- **Commercial co-branding** — You may not use Norm. Marks in product names, logos, or marketing materials without explicit written permission.

## Reporting

If you encounter unauthorized use of the Norm. Marks, please open an issue in this repository or contact the maintainers.
